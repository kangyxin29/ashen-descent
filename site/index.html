THE GAME

A Dark Sanctuary Awaits

Ashen Descent is a dark pixel-art action roguelite set in the ruins of an ancient sanctuary. Each run drops you into a shifting rift where every room could be your last.

02

THE WORLD

A Sanctuary Forged in Ash

Once, the Ember Temple stood as the holiest ground in the realm. Pilgrims came from distant lands to bask in its eternal warmth. Then the Cataclysm struck.

You are not the first to descend. You will not be the last. But perhaps—perhaps you will be the one to restore what was broken.

      Design Philosophy

      Death should feel like a punch to the gut—not a kick to the teeth. The player should think "one more try" not "why bother."

      Painful enough to create tension. Forgiving enough to preserve momentum.

      BOSS ENCOUNTERS

      Face the Guardians

      Each boss is a final exam. Everything you've learned—pattern recognition, build optimization, nerve—tested in one decisive encounter.

      Multi-Phase Battles
